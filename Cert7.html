DataSense Analytics - Microsoft Office Specialist: Excel Associate (MO-200) Certification Training Program



The "Microsoft Office Specialist: Excel Associate (MO-200) Training Program" provided me with comprehensive training and preparation for the MO-200 certification exam, which validates skills in Excel's core functionality. Through this program, I gained proficiency in creating and managing worksheets and workbooks, applying formulas and functions, creating charts and objects, and managing data cells and ranges. The program also equipped me with skills in formatting cells and worksheets, managing tables and table data, and applying conditional logic in worksheets. The training was delivered through a combination of lectures, hands-on exercises, and assessments to reinforce my understanding of the concepts and skills covered.
