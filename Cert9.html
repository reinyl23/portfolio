UnionBank of the Philippines Xcellerator Program - Data Science Development Program



The UnionBank of the Philippines Xcellerator Program - Data Science Development Program was a comprehensive and immersive training program that equipped me with the necessary skills to excel in the field of data science using Python. The program provided me with an in-depth understanding of Python programming concepts, including data structures, algorithms, and object-oriented programming. The program also focused on data science techniques such as data cleaning, data analysis, machine learning, and data visualization. Throughout the program, I worked on real-world projects to gain hands-on experience in applying the concepts and techniques I learned. The program also provided me with mentorship from industry experts who provided guidance and support throughout my learning journey. Through this program, I developed the skills and knowledge to tackle complex data science problems, and I am confident that I can contribute to any organization that requires data-driven decision-making.
