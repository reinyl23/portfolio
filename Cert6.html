PowerBI for Beginners



Through the "Power BI for Beginners" course, I gained foundational knowledge and hands-on experience with Microsoft's powerful business intelligence and data visualization tool. This course equipped me with the skills to import and transform data from multiple sources, create and customize interactive visualizations, and share reports and dashboards with others. I learned how to use the Power BI Desktop application to create reports and the Power BI Service to share these reports with others. Throughout the course, I gained an understanding of the different components of Power BI, including data sources, data modeling, visualizations, and sharing.
